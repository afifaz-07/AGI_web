What is AGI?

AGI is a humanitarian and educational platform dedicated to informing people about historical and ongoing genocides around the world. It combines verified research with survivor voices to educate and inspire responsible action.

Why Does AGI Exist?

Knowledge about genocides is often scattered and unreliable. AGI exists to centralize authentic information, raise awareness, and empower users to understand and act against injustice worldwide.

Learn the Basics

What is Genocide?

Genocide is the deliberate attempt to destroy a group of people based on identity, ethnicity, religion, or culture.
It’s not history — it’s a global crisis that continues to harm millions today.

Stages of Genocide

Genocide unfolds in ten stages — from classification and dehumanization to extermination and denial.
Understanding these stages exposes patterns of hate before they become unstoppable atrocities.

Warning Signs

Genocide doesn’t start with guns — it starts with words, laws, and division.
Watch for propaganda, dehumanization, and exclusion; acting early can save lives.
